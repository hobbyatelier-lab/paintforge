About PaintForge

PaintForge is an independent tool for miniature painters: track what you own, and find the mathematically closest substitute for any paint with IrisMatch — real color science (Delta E 2000), honest about its limits.

Built by one painter who wanted his paint collection to be as organized as his backlog isn't. PaintForge is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any paint manufacturer. All product names, ranges, and trademarks belong to their respective owners and are used for identification only.

Data & credits

Color data comes from two sources: the open-source dataset originally created by Rick Fleuren (Arcturus5404/miniature-paints), used under the MIT License; and manufacturers' official published chip references. Approximately 458 entries were digitally sampled directly from manufacturers' official chip references by the PaintForge team (377 at launch baseline + 81 Warhammer Colour Contrast and Shade entries added post-launch).

On-screen swatches are approximations; finish, opacity, and pigment behaviour aren't captured, and every display renders color differently. Found an error? Tell us — corrections ship fast.
