Privacy Policy

Effective July 10, 2026

The short version: we collect the minimum needed to run the app, we never sell your personal data, we don't show ads, and account deletion is one email away. We do build anonymous market statistics — explained plainly below.

1 · Who we are

PaintForge (paintforge.io) is operated by AYT International S.C. (Mexico). Privacy contact: artificer@paintforge.io

2 · What we collect and why

  • Account data — email and encrypted credentials, to sign you in and reset passwords. Legal basis: contract.
  • Your hobby data — inventory, sets, preferred brands, settings. This is the service. Legal basis: contract.
  • Usage analytics — anonymous-by-default feature events via PostHog EU Cloud, in cookieless mode. Used to understand what's working. Legal basis: legitimate interest. Never used for advertising.
  • Technical basics — IP addresses and browser info processed by infrastructure to deliver pages and prevent abuse.

3 · Aggregate insights

We create anonymous, aggregated statistics from inventory and project activity across all users — for example, how many painters own a given range, or which colors are most commonly missing. These statistics are fully anonymized: they never contain your identity and cannot be traced back to you or your individual collection. We use these insights to improve PaintForge, and we may share or sell them to paint manufacturers and other industry parties. What gets shared is market statistics — never your personal data, never your individual inventory.

4 · What we don't do

No selling of your personal data — ever. No advertising or ad-tracking. No profiling for marketing. Your inventory is yours and nobody else's business; only the anonymous crowd-level statistics above are ever shared.

5 · Where your data lives

Account and inventory data: Supabase, hosted in Singapore (ap-southeast-1); international transfers are covered by Supabase's data processing agreement, including EU standard contractual clauses. Analytics: PostHog EU Cloud. Transactional emails (signup confirmations, password resets) are sent via Resend, which processes your email address on our behalf. All three process data under their GDPR commitments.

6 · Cookies & local storage

Only what's needed to keep you signed in and remember settings. Analytics run in cookieless mode — no tracking cookies. No third-party advertising cookies of any kind.

7 · How long we keep it

Account and inventory data: while your account exists. Database backups: 7 days. Analytics retention: 12 months.

8 · Deleting your account

Email artificer@paintforge.io from your account's email address and we'll delete your account and personal data — typically same day, always within 7 days. Export your inventory from the app before requesting deletion if you want to keep it. Backup copies expire within 7 days. Anonymous aggregate statistics already generated don't contain your identity and aren't affected.

9 · Your rights

Copy, correct, delete, restrict, object, port — email artificer@paintforge.io, no forms, no hoops. You can also complain to your local data-protection authority, though we'd appreciate the chance to fix things first.

10 · Children

PaintForge isn't intended for anyone under 16.

11 · Changes

Material changes announced in the app and the changelog before taking effect.
