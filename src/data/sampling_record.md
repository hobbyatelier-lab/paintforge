# PaintForge — Hex Sampling Record
Canonical log of PaintForge-sampled product lines (manufacturer digital
chips, sampled by Agus). Rule: check here before sampling — never
sample the same line twice, never lose track of what's done.
Updated: July 10, 2026.

Column glossary — Rows: total DB entries in the line (paints + non-
paint products). Paints: entries with a sampled hex (Rows − NULLs).
NULLs: non-paint products (thinners, varnishes, clears, primers,
retarders, accessories) logged with no hex. ⚠approx: paints whose chip
can't be fully represented by a flat hex (metallics, pearls,
iridescents, fluorescents, candy, translucent/transparent) — hex is a
best approximation from pixel sampling.
